FAQs

Some things you might wanna know...

What are the sleeping arrangements?

It will be shared rooms, and a mix of shared beds and singles (sleepover style!) and floor mattresses too - the rooms are massive! We appreciate not everyone has oodles of cash and we wanted to make sure as many of Jenn's faves could afford to come as possible, but if anyone is opposed to sharing a bed or has dibs on who they want to share with, just drop one of us a message and we will accommodate as much as we can - no judgements here - and we promise we'll do what we can (without remodelling the house).

Can you remind me of costs?

The cost per hen is £250 - this includes covering Jenn. This covers:

  • 2 night stay
  • a (simple) breakfast both days
  • dinner the first night
  • pub lunch on Saturday
  • a welcome glass of bubbly (boozy or alcohol-free)
  • planned activities

Please bring anything else you want to drink at the house, and get your own drinks from the bar at the pub.

If we end up coming in under the £250 the extra monies will ofc be refunded to you.

I can't come anymore, can I have my money back?

That really sucks, but unfortunately if you confirm that you are coming and then cancel we won’t be able to refund you. By saying you will be there will affect how much we spend and what we do. Obviously we completely appreciate that things happen and changes might occur, but it will impact everyone else who attends. We really hope that you understand!
